99.99% of startups may think domain name registration as an unavoidable cost, we treat the domain registry as our startup partner. I would like to summarise how HabHub be benefited from Startup League during RISE 2017.

To learn more, please visit https://startupleague.online/ or simply drop me a message.

RISE is one of the major Tech conferences in Asia. Startup League has a strong relationship with RISE. In 2017, Startup League launched a supporting scheme to let startups using one of the 9 top-level domains (e.g. .tech, .online, .space, etc) powered by Radix. The top 5 nominated teams have full sponsorship to join RISE 2017, free T-shirts and bundled with a bouquet of goodies.

Dedicated Facebook post by RISE: Before the launch of the event, RISE Facebook page announced the top 5 teams in a dedicated Facebook post. It means that HabHub is already visible to all visitors before the launch of the event. Indeed, I treated it as an ice-breaking sentence when I pitched to the visitors in the past 2 days.

The booth: In Day 1, everything was prepared and delivered by Startup League, including T-shirts and goodies. I could be focused on pitching visitors. Due to the overwhelming participants, we can only stage in the area in Day 1.

Media coverage: HabHub received an unexpected media coverage. The largest TV broadcaster in Hong Kong, TVB, had an in-depth (~20 minutes) interview with me. The TV show will be shown in a week later. Although the final coverage might last for 30-60 seconds, it is a huge support for HabHub - an early stage startup based in Hong Kong.

Networking opportunities with other startups in Hong Kong and oversea: Getting media coverage is unpredictable, but you MUST be able to get lots of connections. In the past 2 days, I chatted with hundreds of different startup teams based in Hong Kong and oversea. Here is one of the selfies I took during in Day 2.

In Day 2 noon, I took a photo with Suman, the team lead of Startup League. In the past few months, I was very satisfied with the domain registration and hosting services, especially the email service. I sincerely wish to keep a close relationship with this wonderful community in the next few years.
