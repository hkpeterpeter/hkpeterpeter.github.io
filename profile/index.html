Peter Chung

Teaching Associate
Room 2532 via Lift 25/26

Mr Chung has more than 8 years of experience in the higher education sector. He has solid experience in software engineering, entrepreneurship, and alumni engagement. He is an active Hackathon organizer and participant and a co-founder of a HealthTech startup. He maintains strong connections with the local startup community.

Hackathon Expert Since 2014, I partnered with 10+ companies/organizations to organize Hackathon in Hong Kong. In addition, I used to be a judge, a mentor, a participant, and a winning team member of Hackathon. I am now serving as AngelHack Hong Kong Ambassador.

Educator Besides from the teaching support duties, I used to be an advisor at Student Innovation for Global Health Technology (SIGHT), HKUST and a STEM course instructor at Academy of Bright Future Young Engineers (ABFYE), HKUST. I also serve as Advisor in various student interest groups at HKUST.

Active Alumni I am the Executive Sub-Committee Member of Cyberport Startup Alumni Association (CSAA) and the Founding President of Computer Science and Engineering Alumni Association, HKUST (CSEAA)

Entrepreneur I am a co-founder of HabHub, an award winning HealthTech startup at Cyberport
