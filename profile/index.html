Peter Chung

Teaching Associate
Room 2532 via Lift 25/26

cspeter@cse.ust.hk
HEAT = Hackathon Expert + Entrepreneur + Alumni + Teacher

Mr Chung has more than 10 years of experience in the higher education sector. He has solid experience in software engineering, system and kernel programming in Linux, web and mobile app development, entrepreneurship, e-learning, STEM education, student advising, and alumni engagement. Besides from his teaching duties at HKUST, he is an experienced Hackathon organizer in Hong Kong. He is also a Hong Kong Cyberport startup alumnus and maintains close connections with the Hong Kong startup community.

Hackathon Expert He partnered with 10+ companies/ organizations to organize various Hackathon competitions in Hong Kong. He served as Organizer, Consultant, Judge, Speaker, Emcee, and Mentor. In addition, he participated several Hackathon competitions and won a few Hackathon competitions. In particular, he used to turn a Hackathon prototype to a startup project at Cyberport in 2016-17

Entrepreneur He co-founded HabHub, an award-winning HealthTech startup project at Cyberport, Hong Kong in 2016. In addition, he is an alumnus in Entrepreneur First (EF), Hong Kong.

Alumni He co-founded Computer Science and Engineering Alumni Association, HKUST (CSEAA) in 2013. He also served as an Executive Sub-Committee Member in MedTech, Cyberport Startup Alumni Association (CSAA)

Teacher Since 2008, he serves as Teaching Associate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, HKUST. Since 2017, he serves as a STEM Course Instructor at Academy of Bright Future Young Engineers (ABFYE), School of Engineering, HKUST. He served as Advisor at Student Innovation for Global Health Technology (SIGHT), HKUST in 2015-16.
